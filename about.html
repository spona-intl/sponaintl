Who We Are

Global Peer Networks Meet Advanced Brain Science.

SPONA Intl. (The Student-led Peer Outreach and Neuroscience Association) is a global academic network run entirely by students, for students. We empower young minds to dive into neuroscience, conduct peer-led research, and connect with an international community of future scientists.

Disclaimer: All content shared is strictly for informational and educational purposes and does not constitute professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Our Mission

Demystify the Brain

Making complex neuroscience and academic research accessible, engaging, and relevant to secondary school students worldwide.

Global Peer Support

Providing an inclusive, digital space where students can connect, share academic insights, and collaborate on scientific projects.

Digital Security & Privacy Statement

Zero-Data Platform Policy

As an international organization built by students, digital trust is our highest priority. We operate under strict digital integrity structures:

  • No Personal Tracking: We do not collect, track, or store any personal visitor data or analytical telemetry.
  • No Financial Details: Our platform is 100% non-commercial. We never request financial details or payments.
  • Safe Open Access: All articles and research toolkits are completely open and free to access safely anywhere.
