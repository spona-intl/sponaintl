🌐 Who We Are

SPONA International

The Student-led Peer Outreach and Neuroscience Association is a global, student-led academic network. We bridge the gap between youth peer collaboration and the cutting-edge world of brain science.

Run entirely by students, for students, our network empowers young minds to dive into neuroscience, conduct peer-led research, and connect with an international community of future scientists.

🚀 Our Mission

  • Demystify the Brain

    Making complex neuroscience and research accessible, engaging, and relevant to secondary school students worldwide.

  • Global Peer Support

    Providing an inclusive, online space where students can connect, share academic insights, and collaborate on scientific projects.

  • Youth-Led Innovation
